Cooking Beef Liver

First quality is everything with taste. Buying from a farm where the animal has been pastured for life is a completely different food than buyinga store bought liver; from the bacteria content to the nutrition.

Another tip is to add the liver into the freezer for few weeks before cooking. That, when cooked seems to reduce the bitter taste - especially from shop bought produce.

Ingredients

  • Liver
  • salt

Steps

  1. Get the pan very hot
  2. Put the liver in for 30 seconds
  3. turn over and do the same

it is important to keep the middle raw, that is where the sweet taste resides, this way you do not get that bittertaste that puts off lots of people.

Enjoy!
