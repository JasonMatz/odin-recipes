Cod Liver Recipe

Now it is quite hard to find this in physicals shops, at least in UK.

Eastern European shops usually have it. It is pure Cod Liverina can. Not only is it buttery Deliscious, but it is packed with omega-3 fatty acids, you could call it a supplement in a can

Ingredients

  • Money
  • Internet connection
  • Preferably a maps app

Steps

  1. Have around £2/£3
  2. Search online for 'canned cod liver'
  3. Go get your Liver!

Cod liver tastes very buttery and almost sweet, but the texture can be overpowering and just too fatty. One suggestion is to have it with toast or a cheese sandwitch, this balances the liver out.
